HOW TO READ: Each row is one symbol (not one strike). ATM CE+PE options are averaged across expiries to give one signal per underlying.
⬆ BULL HEDGE — Spot went UP but PE (put) premium also went UP. Smart money buying puts on a rally — bearish hedge / distribution signal.
⬇ BEAR SQUEEZE — Spot went DOWN but CE (call) premium went UP. Call buying on a dip — potential short squeeze or bullish reversal bet.
⚡ IV SPIKE — Spot barely moved but IV jumped ≥1%. Event anticipation — earnings, results, news not yet public.
🔴 SMART SELL — Spot UP but CE premium DOWN. Call sellers active on rally — strong resistance / ceiling.
🟢 SMART BUY — Spot DOWN but PE premium DOWN. Put sellers active on dip — strong support / floor signal.
MAGNITUDE = |spot%| + |premium%| — higher = stronger divergence.
MODE: SNAPSHOT = last 2 OC snapshots (minutes apart). INTRADAY = today's first snapshot vs latest.
